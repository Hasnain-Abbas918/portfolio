How to Publish Your Portfolio Online for Free

Follow these step-by-step instructions to make your portfolio website accessible to anyone on the internet.

1

Download and Customize

After downloading the portfolio files, open the HTML file in a text editor and replace the placeholder content with your information.

2

Choose a Free Hosting Platform

Select one of these excellent free hosting services:

GitHub Pages

Best for developers, integrates with Git

Netlify

Easiest to use, drag-and-drop deployment

Vercel

Great for frontend projects, fast deployment

3

Create an Account

Sign up for a free account on your chosen platform.

  • GitHub: github.com
  • Netlify: netlify.com
  • Vercel: vercel.com
4

Upload Your Website

Each platform has slightly different upload methods:

Netlify (Easiest):
  • Log in to Netlify
  • Drag and drop your "index.html" file to the deployment area
  • Your site will be live instantly with a random URL
GitHub Pages:
  • Create a new repository named "username.github.io" (replace username with your GitHub username)
  • Upload your HTML file to the repository
  • Go to Settings → Pages → Select "main" branch → Save
  • Your site will be available at https://username.github.io
5

Customize Your URL

Most platforms allow you to customize your site's URL:

  • Netlify: Go to Site settings → Change site name
  • GitHub Pages: Your URL is always username.github.io
  • Consider buying a custom domain (e.g., yourname.com) for a more professional look
6

Share Your Portfolio

Once your site is live, share the URL with potential employers, clients, and on your social media profiles.

Remember: Update your portfolio content regularly with new projects and experiences.

Updating Your Published Site

To update your portfolio after it's published:

  • Netlify: Simply drag and drop the updated HTML file
  • GitHub Pages: Upload the updated file to your repository
  • Changes typically go live within 1-5 minutes
